• Audio playback for meditation/sleep.
• Local reminders via notifications.

Tech Stack*

• Backend: Firebase
• Database: Firestore
• State: Redux
• Notifications: OneSignal

• PascalCase components; camelCase vars/functions.
• Modular folders: screens/components/redux.
• Clean, reusable, documented code.
• No redundant imports/assets.
